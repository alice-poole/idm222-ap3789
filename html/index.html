Résumé

Design for all.

As a woman living with disability and a proud member of the LGBTQIA+ community, I know inclusion matters.

Accessibility is important. This very site uses patterned backgrounds for colorblind accessibility, and button-styled links for screen readers!

As a lifelong nerd, I love making games and websites easy to use for more people. That's why I'm passionate about UX, UI, and game design.
